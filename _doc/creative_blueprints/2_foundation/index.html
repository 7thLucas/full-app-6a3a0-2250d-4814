The Problem

Simple payments buried under wallets, fake cashback, and 15-tap flows

Existing payment super apps force users through wallet top-ups, fake balance mechanics, and bloated UX to do what should take three taps. The design locks money in — it doesn't move it fast.

The Solution

FlowPay — straight to Razorpay, no detours

Select service → enter amount → Razorpay checkout → done. No wallet. No stored money. No fake cashback. Real transactions only, every time — with a pluggable Flutter architecture built to expand into recharge, bills, taxi, food, and courier.
